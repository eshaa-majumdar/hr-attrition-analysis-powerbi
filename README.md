# HR Attrition Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes employee attrition using the IBM HR Analytics dataset.

The objective was to identify the employee groups most likely to leave and provide recommendations to improve retention.

## Tools Used

- Power BI
- Excel
- DAX
- Data Cleaning
- Business Analysis

## Business Question

Why are employees leaving, and which employee segments are at highest risk of attrition?

## Key KPIs

- Total Employees: 1,470
- Total Attritions: 237
- Attrition Rate: 16%

## Key Findings

- Sales had the highest attrition rate.
- Research & Development accounted for the largest number of departures.
- Overtime showed a strong relationship with attrition.
- Employees with lower income levels were more likely to leave.
- Attrition was highest among younger employees and those with short tenure.


## Recommendations

1. Monitor overtime and workload.
2. Review compensation for lower-income employees.
3. Improve onboarding and support for new hires.
